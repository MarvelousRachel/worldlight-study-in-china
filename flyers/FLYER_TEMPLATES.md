## Quick quality rules (for every flyer)

1. Keep **one main message** per flyer.
2. Use **big WhatsApp number** (largest text after headline).
3. Avoid “guarantee” language.
4. Include **intake month/year** if it’s time-sensitive.
5. Use consistent colors (match site: purple/blue + green accents).

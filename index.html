Intakes & timelines

China universities usually have two main intakes. Exact dates depend on the school and program. Chat with us and we’ll tell you what’s open now.

Spring intake

Often starts around Feb / Mar (varies by university).

  • Good for some language programs and selected majors
  • Earlier document preparation is best

Autumn intake

Often starts around Sep (most programs available).

  • Most common intake for degree programs
  • Scholarship timelines can be longer

FAQ

Quick answers. Add your exact policies for clarity.

Do you guarantee a scholarship?

No. Scholarship decisions are made by universities and scholarship bodies. We provide guidance and application support.

What documents are usually required?

Commonly: passport, highest diploma, transcripts, study plan, CV, recommendation letters, and medical form (varies by program/university).

How long does the process take?

Timelines differ by intake and university. Many applications take several weeks to months.

How much does it cost to use WorldLight?

Our fees depend on the service package and program level. Contact us on WhatsApp for the current pricing and what's included. We'll give you a clear breakdown before you commit.

Which countries can apply?

We work with students from across Africa, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and beyond. If you're unsure whether your country qualifies, message us and we'll check for you.

Can I apply if I don't speak Chinese?

Yes. Many programs in China are taught in English, especially at the bachelor's and master's level in medicine, engineering, and business. We can also guide you on Chinese language programs if you'd like to learn first.

How do I check my scholarship or application status?

We follow up on your behalf and keep you updated. You can also message us on WhatsApp at any time for a status update — WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us.

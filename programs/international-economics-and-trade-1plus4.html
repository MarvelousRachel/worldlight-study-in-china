Basic information

  • Field: Foundation Course
  • Program: International Economics and Trade (1+4)
  • Degree: 5 years (Non-degree track)
  • Intake: 2026 Autumn
  • Application deadline: Jun 30, 2026
  • Teaching language: Chinese

This program is a 1-year preparatory (foundation) + 4-year undergraduate track. Progression may require meeting HSK requirements.

Scholarship information

Scholarship type: Type C · Duration: 5 years

Disbursement method: Students pay required fees first, then the scholarship is released by the school after enrollment and eligibility.

Scholarship policy (tuition based on GPA)

  • Original tuition (preparatory): 14,000 RMB/year
  • Original tuition (undergraduate): 14,000 RMB/year
  • GPA 4.0–5.0: 100% scholarship → tuition after scholarship: 0 RMB/year
  • GPA 3.0–3.99: 80% scholarship → tuition after scholarship: 2,800 RMB/year
  • GPA 2.0–2.99: 60% scholarship → tuition after scholarship: 5,600 RMB/year
  • GPA 1.0–1.99: 40% scholarship → tuition after scholarship: 8,400 RMB/year

Note: Scholarship is typically issued within one month once awarded (based on policy).

Application requirements

  • Age: 18–25
  • Accept students who have been to China: Yes
  • Minors accepted: No
  • Current location: Unlimited
Some countries may not be eligible for this program due to high application volume. (We can confirm eligibility during application.)

Application documents

  • Passport-sized photo
  • Passport ID page
  • Academic transcripts (color scan)
  • Highest degree diploma (color scan)
  • Foreigner physical examination form (incl. blood reports, within 3 months)
  • Non-criminal record (within 3 months)
  • Bank statement (≥ $3000, dated within last 30 days)
  • Application form + university application form
  • Study plan
  • CV (must include email + phone)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Residence permit / study certificate in China (if applicable)
  • Other documents (if required)

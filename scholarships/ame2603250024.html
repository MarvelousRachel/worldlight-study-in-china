Scholarship policy Open

1 — Original tuition fee: 20000 RMB/year.

2 — Scholarship policy: (Scholarships cannot be combined)

(1) University Scholarship: each year you can apply for a scholarship equivalent to the tuition fee, free accommodation, and a stipend (600 RMB/month for science & engineering, 300 RMB/month for humanities; 10 months/year; paid monthly).

(2) Provincial Government Scholarship (Category A): can cover tuition and accommodation fees, implemented according to the policy issued by the provincial department.

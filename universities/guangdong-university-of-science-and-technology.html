About Guangdong University of Science and Technology

Guangdong University of Science and Technology was founded in 2003. It is a full-time undergraduate institution approved by the Ministry of Education, with engineering as its main discipline and coordinated development of management, economics, literature, art and other disciplines.

It is located in Dongguan (Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area) and has two campuses, covering more than 2,000 mu. The institution was upgraded to a bachelor’s degree-granting university in 2011 and continues to expand its programs and industry partnerships.

Since its establishment, the school has pursued an applied, innovation-oriented approach to talent development. It offers dozens of undergraduate programs and works with major industry partners.
